# Advocate Ajay Kumar Singh - Lawyer Website

This is a **professional, responsive website** for Advocate Ajay Kumar Singh, designed for showcasing legal services, providing legal notes, and allowing clients to contact the advocate directly.

---

## **Live Demo**

Visit the live website here:  
[https://krishnabhardwaj8303-sys.github.io/Lawyer-Website/](https://krishnabhardwaj8303-sys.github.io/Lawyer-Website/)

---

## **Features**

- **Responsive Design** – Works perfectly on mobile, tablet, and desktop.  
- **Hero Section** – Attractive landing section with lawyer’s name and tagline.  
- **Services Section** – Highlights Civil, Criminal, Property, and Family Law services.  
- **Clients / Partners Section** – Showcases logos or images of clients.  
- **Legal Notes Section** – Free and premium downloadable notes in PDF format.  
- **Premium Notes** – Option for clients to pay via WhatsApp before downloading.  
- **Contact Form** – Clients can contact via email or WhatsApp directly.  
- **Floating Buttons** – Quick WhatsApp and YouTube access.  
- **Google Maps Integration** – Displays office location.  

---

## **Technologies Used**

- **HTML5**  
- **CSS3 (Responsive + Media Queries)**  
- **JavaScript** – For contact form and premium notes payment alert.  
- **GitHub Pages** – Hosting the website for free.
